Your Data in Your Cloud

When you are the original person to upload a media item (e.g., an image or a URL), that gets stored as files in your cloud storage. For example, if you sign in with Google Drive, and then upload a new image, that image and its associated comment file gets stored in your Google Drive. If you add a comment to another persons originally uploaded media item, that gets added to the other person's comment file.

Social Users

If you invite someone who doesn't have cloud storage to share your album (e.g., an Apple or Facebook user), then any original uploads they do will get stored in your cloud storage.
