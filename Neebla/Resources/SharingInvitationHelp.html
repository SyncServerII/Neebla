• You are sharing a link to an album: Upon accepting the invitation, the invited people will get updates in Neebla whenever the album gets updated. People can accept using Dropbox or Google accounts and (optionally) Apple or Facebook accounts when you allow social acceptance.
  • Number of people to invite: The sharing invitation will be sent using email (just tap on "Invite" above to add email addresses). You can send the invitation to this many people via email or post to a social media channel, and this many people will be able to redeem it. The default is one person. You can allow up to 10 people to accept the invitation.
  • Permissions
    • Read-only: The invited person (or people) can only download media & discussions. They don't get to add new media or make new comments.
    • Read & Change: The invited person (or people) can also add new media and make new comments. (This is the default).
    • Read, Change, & Invite: The invited person (or people) can also invite other people to the share the album. They can also change the name of the album.
  • Allow social acceptance? If you allow this, then Apple or Facebook user(s) can accept the invitation. If the permissions you give allow them to add new media, then these media and discussion files will be stored in your cloud storage (or the original owning user's cloud storage). The default does not allow social acceptance.
  • Number of days before invitation expires: This lets you select how long the invitations last before expiring. The default is three days. The minimum is one day and the maximum is seven days.
